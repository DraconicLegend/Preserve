Partners

See Who We're Working With

Robbie Guimond:

Robbie Guimond cleared Phase 1 of the invasives and prepared the site for planting. We could not have started this project without Robbie.

Bartlett Tree Experts

Bartlett Tree Experts removed four invasive Tree of Heaven. Tree of Heaven spread rapidly and are host to the destructive Spotted Lanternfly.

AJ Penna and Son:

AJ Penna and Son dug the holes for the native trees. In this very rocky New England soil, this was a difference maker.

Connecticut Audubon:

Connecticut Audubon provided start up funds and guidance.

Aspetuck Land Trust

Aspetuck Land Trust donated trees, shrubs and wildflowers.
